Early memes (First meme - 2008)

Pre 2010 memes around this time were innocent and silly. Mostly. Most of them consisted of viral images or videos being sent to you, your friends and family. This period is when the inside joke aspect of the meme comes in. It was more personal back then, when it would only be between people you have good or strong connections with. Twitter had only started in 2006 and YouTube 2005, just a year earlier, so memes were just beginning to get injected into people’s blood streams. Memes were just getting popular, but it didn’t have an iconic image associated with it. That was until the creation memes.
