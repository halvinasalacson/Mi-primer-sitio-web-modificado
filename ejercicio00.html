Meme culture

This page serves as a guide to help inform the unenlightened of the expressive and meaningful art that is memes.

Definitions

What is a meme? Is it an image? An icon? A celebrity? A meme is an art, idea or behaviour that is spread from person to person. A meme carries cultural meaning or symbolism that provokes a particular response from individuals Memes come from something. Something relatable like an inside joke or an experience we’ve had. A person sends a meme because everyone else will know exactly what the person meant. That is what memes mean now in modern times – a way of sharing funny ideas without needing to talk to each other.
